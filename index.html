Adaptive Practice: While your child practices personalized math problems, Zeal’s algorithm automatically adapts to their skill level, identifies knowledge gaps, and determines what they are ready to learn next.

One-On-One Tutoring: While your child practices, a tutor can join them for one-on-one tutoring. The tutor and your child work together using a shared whiteboard and 2-way audio feed. They break down a problem specifically selected for your child to develop a deeper understanding of the topic. Then your child is retested on the skills they just worked to see how much they’ve just learned!

Personalized Feedback: Your child receives feedback and live help while using Zeal. You will get weekly progress report emails that include personalized feedback from your child’s tutors, video recordings of the tutoring sessions, and details on the skills your scholar learned! In addition, included in Zeal is a weekly Parent-Tutor check in call to discuss your child’s progress on Zeal!
